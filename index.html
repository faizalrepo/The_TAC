Rules & Regulations.


1. This contest is open to every student of the Artificial Intelligence and Data Science department on an individual basis.
2. Contestants can use any kind of designing tools and even AI generators to create their designs.
3. Contestants can also sketch their design in a paper and can submit it on the website.
4. The participants must submit the final work only in the given format (template).Can be downloaded from the website.
5. For the participants using photoshop, the template can be downloaded in the form of a .psd file.
6. For those using other designing tools, a .png file can be downloaded from the web page and those participants must design the templates themself.
7. The logo must be the original work of the submitter.And not infringe upon anyone else's intellectual property.
8. Final work must be submitted in the form of .pdf, .jpeg, .psd, .jpg
9. The selected logo will be considered and the idea will be taken for the TAC official logo.




Template Details.


1. The participants must submit the final work only in the given format of the template.
2. This template is a simple mood board through which you can throw your creativity and inspire us.
3. Participants must submit their completed work through this website
4. Typeface -This describes the fonts used in your design. One primary and secondary font should be used. At Least 2 or more fonts can be used.
5. Colour - One or 2 colours can be used of your choice for your design.
6. Words - A space to present any Motto for TAC.
7. Mockup - An exact replica of how the logo design would look on something like for example a T-Shirt, a Water Bottle, a Cap or anything else.
8. Pattern, Vibe/Energy - Upto your creativity.

Submission.


1. Competition starts on September 18,2023 at 05:00 PM
2. Submissions must be received by 11:59 PM on September 24, 2023.
3. Submission must be done only through the website.
